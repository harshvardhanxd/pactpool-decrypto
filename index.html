How PactPool Works

1

Choose Tier & Lock ETH

Bronze, Silver, or Gold — higher tier means higher penalty risk AND bigger share of the penalty pool when others fail. Set your goal, category, and lock duration.

2

Go Public on the Goal Wall

Your goal appears publicly, tagged by category. The community can see your commitment. Accountability built directly into the protocol.

3

Complete = Claim Bonus

Reach your unlock date and withdraw your full ETH plus a proportional share of the penalty pool — funded by everyone who gave up early.

4

Break Early = Fund Winners

Withdraw early and pay a 10–30% dynamic penalty. That ETH flows on-chain into the reward pool for disciplined savers. No middleman. No exceptions.
